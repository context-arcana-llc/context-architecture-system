# Retail Subscription Program

Subscription is the heart of retail. Predictable revenue, lower cost-to-acquire than market customers, and gives us a reason to source interesting micro-lots that wouldn't make sense at wholesale scale.

---

## Tiers

| Tier | Frequency | Quantity | Price / delivery | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Weekly | Every 7 days | 1 × 12oz bag | $22 | Mostly local pickup |
| Biweekly | Every 14 days | 1 × 12oz bag | $22 | Most common tier |
| Monthly | Every 28 days | 2 × 12oz bags | $40 | Best value, most popular shipped tier |
| Sampler | Every 28 days | 3 × 4oz bags | $30 | Different origins each month, no repeats |

All tiers ship USPS Priority Mail (2-3 day) within continental US. Local pickup at the mill or Saturday market is free; pickups credited $4 per delivery.

---

## What's included

- Coffee bag(s) — whole bean by default; ground if requested at signup
- A 1-page card with origin notes, roast date, and brewing suggestions
- Free shipping over $40 cart total (so Monthly + Sampler ship free; Weekly/Biweekly add $6)

---

## Pause and cancel

- Pause anytime from account page, no minimum
- Cancel anytime, no fees
- No commitment beyond the next scheduled delivery

## What this offer is NOT

- **Not a "coffee of the month" club.** We're consistent, not novelty-driven.
- **Not gift subscriptions.** Those are separate SKUs in Shopify with their own SKU notes.
- **Not a wholesale-style account.** See `context/03_offers/wholesale-program.md` for wholesale terms.

---

## Pricing rationale

Tiers are priced so the Monthly tier is the clear value choice (effectively $20/bag vs. $22/bag at lower tiers). This is intentional — fewer fulfillment touchpoints per dollar of revenue. We want subscribers to land on Monthly.
