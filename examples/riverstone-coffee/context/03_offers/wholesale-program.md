# Wholesale Program

70% of revenue. Cafes, restaurants, and one office in Hollingford and the surrounding region (everything within ~90 minutes of the mill).

---

## Account structure

| Tier | Volume / month | Pricing | Terms |
|---|---|---|---|
| Standard | 10–40 lb | $14 / lb | Net-15, prepay first order |
| Growing | 40–100 lb | $13 / lb | Net-30, prepay first order |
| Established | 100+ lb | $12 / lb | Net-30 |

Tier reviewed quarterly based on rolling 3-month average. Accounts moving up get the new pricing on next order; accounts moving down get one quarter's notice.

---

## What's included

- 5 lb bags (whole bean unless requested otherwise)
- Free delivery within 30 miles; flat $25 fee for 30–90 mile delivery; standard freight beyond that
- Custom roast profile available at Established tier
- Bag labels include roast date and origin

---

## What's NOT included

- Equipment loans — we're not big enough
- Co-branded bags — see `context/07_decisions/` for current thinking (TBD)
- Exclusive territories — we don't restrict accounts geographically

## Pricing rationale

Target gross margin: 55% at Standard, 50% at Growing, 45% at Established. The volume discount is intentional — we want to reward consistency, not optimize per-bag margin. We'd rather have 10 Growing accounts ordering monthly than 30 Standard accounts ordering sporadically.
