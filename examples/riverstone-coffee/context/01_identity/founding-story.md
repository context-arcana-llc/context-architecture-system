# Founding Story

Riverstone started in late 2023 when the old grain mill on River Street came up for lease. Mara had been roasting for a local café for three years and wanted her own space. The mill had good ventilation, three-phase power for the roaster, and enough room to grow into.

The first six months were green coffee on credit and a borrowed Probat 5kg. By spring 2024 we had four wholesale accounts in town and a Saturday booth at the farmer's market. Subscriptions came later — a customer at the market kept asking if we shipped, so eventually we did.

The name "Riverstone" comes from the smooth granite stones along the riverbed below the mill. Local, durable, shaped by water. The logo is a simplified version of one of those stones.

We're still in the same building. Still a 5kg roaster (a 12kg arrives in June 2026 — see `context/07_decisions/2026-03_drum-roaster-upgrade.md`). Still Tuesday roasting, Wednesday bagging, Thursday shipping.
