# 01 — Identity

Who you are and how you present to the world.

**What belongs here:**
- Brand voice and tone guidelines
- Mission, vision, and values statements
- Founding story or personal narrative
- Bio and professional positioning
- Visual identity references (logo descriptions, color palette, typography — not the files themselves unless they're small)

**What does not belong here:**
- Client-facing sales material (→ `03_offers/`)
- Finished marketing copy (→ `05_assets/`)
