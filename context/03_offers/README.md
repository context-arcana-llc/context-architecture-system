# 03 — Offers

What you sell, offer, or deliver, and how it's positioned.

**What belongs here:**
- Product and service descriptions
- Pricing structures and tier definitions
- Positioning statements and differentiators
- Offer framing and objection handling notes
- Package or bundle definitions

**What does not belong here:**
- Confidential client pricing that varies by deal (→ `04_clients/`)
- Marketing copy and finished assets (→ `05_assets/`)
