# 07 — Decisions

A record of significant decisions and the reasoning behind them.

**What belongs here:**
- Decision records for choices that were hard to make or that future-you needs to understand
- Architectural, strategic, or operational decisions
- Records of paths not taken and why

**Use the template:** `templates/decision-record.md`

**What does not belong here:**
- Trivial or easily reversible choices
- Task lists or to-dos (those belong in your project management tool)

**Why this matters:** The reasoning behind a decision decays faster than the decision itself. Writing it down while it's fresh is the whole point of this folder.
